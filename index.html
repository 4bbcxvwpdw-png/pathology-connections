How to play

Find four groups of four.

Sixteen pathology terms sit in the grid. Hidden inside are four groups of four that share a theme. Tap four tiles you think belong together, then Submit. Guess all four groups before you run out of four mistakes. Groups get harder from ochre to brick, and some terms look like they fit more than one group, so watch for traps. Some tiles are images: tap the magnifier to enlarge a slide. When you finish, you'll see a short explanation of every group.
